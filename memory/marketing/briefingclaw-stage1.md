# BriefingClaw — Stage 1 Marketing Plan

**Created:** February 19, 2026
**Status:** Draft

---

## Target Persona

**Primary:** Analysts, researchers, executives, professionals who need daily intelligence, news junkies, knowledge workers

**Demographics:**
- Age: 28-60
- Technical: Moderate to high (values information synthesis, time efficiency)
- Values: Being informed, competitive intelligence, staying ahead, efficiency
- Pain points: Information overload, FOMO, time-consuming research, missed important updates

**Psychographic Profile:**
- "I need to know what's happening before others do" mindset
- Consumes multiple news sources, reports, newsletters daily
- Values curated, synthesized information over raw data
- Time-constrained but information-hungry
- May prepare briefings for teams or leadership

---

## Core Messaging Wedge

### The Problem
You wake up to 47 newsletters, 23 news alerts, and a mountain of reports. By the time you've read what matters, half your morning is gone. You're informed, but at what cost? And did you miss the thing that actually mattered?

### The Solution
**BriefingClaw: Your daily intelligence distilled.**

- Curated briefings from your chosen sources
- AI-synthesized summaries of what matters to you
- Customizable topics, industries, and sources
- Delivered when you want it, how you want it
- Runs locally — your reading habits stay private

### The Hook
> *"Know everything. Read nothing."*
