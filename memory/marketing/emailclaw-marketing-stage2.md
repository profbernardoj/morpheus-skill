## Core Value Proposition

### Primary Positioning
**"Email on your terms. Not its terms."**

EmailClaw is the AI assistant for email — smart triage, follow-up tracking, attachment organization, and draft assistance.

### Value Proposition Statement
> Your inbox is a disaster. 47 unread emails from today alone. Important messages buried under newsletters. Follow-ups you forgot.
> 
> Connect EmailClaw and get an AI that:
> - Prioritizes and triages your inbox automatically
> - Tracks follow-ups and reminds you when promised
> - Finds any attachment from any email instantly
> - Suggests responses and drafts for you
> - Runs locally — your email stays private

## X/Twitter / LinkedIn Content Strategy

### Account Setup
- **Handle:** @EmailClaw
- **Bio:** "AI for email. Smart triage. Follow-up tracking. Attachment search. emailclaw.com"
- **Profile Image:** EmailClaw logo (clean, productive)
- **Header:** Inbox organization visualization

### Posting Cadence
- **4-5 posts per day**
- **Best times:** 8-10 AM EST (morning email check), 1-3 PM EST, 5-7 PM EST
- **Focus:** Productivity, inbox zero, email tips

### Content Pillars

#### Pillar 1: Inbox Chaos (30%)
Relatable email overwhelm, "47 unread" moments.

#### Pillar 2: Triage Tips (30%)
Smart prioritization, follow-up strategies.

#### Pillar 3: Features (25%)
Attachment search, draft assistance, follow-up tracking.

#### Pillar 4: Privacy (15%)
Your email stays local.
