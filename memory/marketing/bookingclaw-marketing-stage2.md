## Core Value Proposition

### Primary Positioning
**"Travel should be an adventure. Not a spreadsheet."**

BookingClaw is the AI assistant for travel — unified itineraries, smart recommendations, document organization, proactive alerts.

### Value Proposition Statement
> Your trip is scattered across 5 confirmation emails, 3 booking apps, and a spreadsheet you haven't updated since January. You forgot the hotel address.
> 
> Connect BookingClaw and get an AI that:
> - Unifies all bookings into one itinerary
> - Provides smart recommendations for your destination
> - Organizes travel documents (passports, visas, confirmations)
> - Alerts you to flight changes and important times
> - Runs locally — your travel data stays private

## X/Twitter / Instagram Content Strategy

### Account Setup
- **Handle:** @BookingClaw
- **Bio:** "AI for travelers. Unified itineraries. Smart recommendations. Document organization. bookingclaw.com"
- **Profile Image:** BookingClaw logo (travel aesthetic)
- **Header:** Travel/destination visualization

### Posting Cadence
- **3-4 posts per day**
- **Best times:** 12-2 PM EST, 7-9 PM EST
- **Focus:** Travel tips, organization, destination inspiration

### Content Pillars

#### Pillar 1: Travel Chaos (35%)
Scattered confirmations, forgotten details, spreadsheet struggles.

#### Pillar 2: Organization (30%)
Unified itineraries, document organization, proactive alerts.

#### Pillar 3: Inspiration (25%)
Destination recommendations, travel tips.

#### Pillar 4: Privacy (10%)
Travel data stays private.

### Sample Posts

**Chaos:**
> 5 confirmation emails. 3 booking apps. 1 spreadsheet from January.
> 
> Where's the hotel address again?
> 
> BookingClaw: "Here's your complete itinerary"
> 
> Travel organized.

**Organization:**
> Flight cancelled.
> 
> → missed the email
> → arrived at airport confused
> 
> BookingClaw: "Flight change alert. Here are your options."
> 
> Be informed. Not surprised.

**Inspiration:**
> Going to Tokyo? BookingClaw recommends:
> - Best ramen spots (based on your preferences)
> - Hidden temples (off the tourist path)
> - JR Pass tips

**Privacy:**
> Your travel plans reveal:
> - Where you live
> - When you're away
> - Your habits
> 
> Keep it private.
