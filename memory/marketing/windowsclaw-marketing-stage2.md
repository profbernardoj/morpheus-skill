## Core Value Proposition

### Primary Positioning
**"AI that speaks fluent Windows."**

WindowsClaw is the AI assistant for Windows users — WSL2 integration, PowerShell automation, Microsoft ecosystem, enterprise-ready.

### Value Proposition Statement
> You work on Windows. Maybe WSL2 for dev, PowerShell for automation, OneDrive for storage. Most AI is built for Mac or Linux and doesn't understand your environment.
> 
> Connect WindowsClaw and get an AI that:
> - Integrates deeply with Windows and WSL2
> - Works with PowerShell for automation
> - Connects to Microsoft ecosystem (Outlook, Teams, OneDrive)
> - Deploys enterprise-ready across organizations
> - Runs locally — your Windows data stays on your machine
