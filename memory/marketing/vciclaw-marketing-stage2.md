## Core Value Proposition

### Primary Positioning
**"See 100 deals. Know 100 stories. Decide in minutes."**

VCIClaw is the AI assistant for venture capitalists — founder research, market analysis, deal memos, and reference prep.

### Value Proposition Statement
> You see 100+ decks a week. Each needs founder research, market analysis, competitive landscape. Associates are drowning in due diligence.
> 
> Connect VCIClaw and get an AI that:
> - Synthesizes founder backgrounds from LinkedIn, Crunchbase, news
> - Generates market and competitive landscape analysis
> - Writes deal memos from your notes
> - Prepares reference call questions tailored to each founder
> - Runs locally — your deal flow data stays confidential

## LinkedIn Content Strategy (Primary Channel)

**LinkedIn over X for VCs**

### Account Setup
- **Page:** VCIClaw (Company Page)
- **Tagline:** Venture Intelligence at Scale

### Posting Cadence
- **2-3 posts per week** (Professional audience, lower frequency)
- **Best times:** Tuesday-Thursday, 8-10 AM EST
- **Focus:** Due diligence, deal intelligence, founder research

### Content Pillars

#### Pillar 1: Due Diligence (35%)
Founder vetting, market analysis, competitive landscape.

#### Pillar 2: Deal Intelligence (30%)
Deal memos, automated research, reference prep.

#### Pillar 3: Efficiency (20%)
Time savings, associate productivity, workflow optimization.

#### Pillar 4: Privacy (15%)
Confidential deal flow, local processing.
